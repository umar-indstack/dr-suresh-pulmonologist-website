Advanced Diagnostic and Treatment Services

Pulmonary Function Test

Pulmonary Function Test (PFT) assesses lung function by measuring airflow, volume, and gas exchange, aiding in diagnosing respiratory conditions.

Allergy Testing (SPT)

Allergy Testing identifies allergens triggering reactions by applying small amounts to the skin and monitoring for a response.

Sleep Study

Sleep Study monitors sleep patterns, breathing, and brain activity to diagnose sleep disorders such as sleep apnea and insomnia.

Bronchoscopy

Bronchoscopy is a procedure to examine airways using a thin, flexible tube with a camera, aiding in diagnosing lung conditions.

Chest Imaging

Advanced chest X-rays and CT scans for accurate diagnosis of lung and respiratory conditions.

Respiratory Infection Care

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of various respiratory infections including pneumonia and bronchitis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions about Pulmonology

What does a pulmonologist treat?

Pulmonologists specialize in diagnosing and treating diseases related to the respiratory system, including conditions like asthma, bronchitis, pneumonia, COPD, tuberculosis, and sleep disorders. Book an appointment at 7200603928.

When should I see a pulmonologist in Kanchipuram?

You should consider seeing a pulmonologist if you experience persistent symptoms such as shortness of breath, chronic cough, wheezing, chest pain, or difficulty breathing during sleep.

How do I make an appointment with a pulmonologist in Kanchipuram?

You can schedule an appointment by calling 7200603928 or using the appointment form on this website. We will be happy to assist you.

What should I expect during my first visit to a pulmonologist?

During your initial visit, the pulmonologist will conduct a thorough medical history review, perform a physical examination, and may order diagnostic tests such as pulmonary function tests, chest X-rays, or allergy testing depending on your condition.

What are the common diagnostic tests performed by pulmonologists?

Common tests include spirometry, pulmonary function tests, bronchoscopy, chest CT scans, arterial blood gas analysis, allergy testing, and sleep studies, depending on the suspected condition.

Can pulmonologists treat sleep disorders?

Yes, pulmonologists specialize in sleep medicine and can diagnose and treat sleep disorders such as sleep apnea, insomnia, and other breathing-related sleep problems.

What are the treatment options for asthma?

Treatment for asthma may include medications such as inhalers (bronchodilators and corticosteroids), allergy medications, and lifestyle changes to avoid triggers. Book an appointment at 7200603928 for personalized treatment.

How is tuberculosis diagnosed and treated?

Tuberculosis is diagnosed through a combination of medical history, physical examination, imaging tests, and laboratory tests. Treatment typically involves a course of antibiotics taken over several months under expert supervision. Book an appointment at 7200603928.
